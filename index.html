Frequently Asked Questions

How do freelancers receive a client booking?
When a consumer maps a request on our connected booking channels, the JMCY matching log algorithms cross-reference available therapists in the vicinity. Selected professionals instantly receive a push notification alert on their network web dashboard containing the date, schedule time, and targeted city area.
What is required to return to active status and receive new bookings after a session?
Upon fulfilling a massage treatment, log into your freelancer portal board and select the 'Mark Service as Completed' button. This closes out the current booking trail, prompts the system to log the 20% platform fee remittance due, and automatically re-flags your system profile as 'Available' to receive fresh incoming local dispatches.
What are the core requirements to join the platform?
Applicants must present a valid government-issued ID and valid proof of massage therapy training or certification.
How does the payout mechanism distribute earnings?
There is no platform waiting period or delay. The consumer pays your entire session fee directly to you via Cash, GCash, or Maya upon arrival or service completion. Afterwards, you remit the 20% network commission allocation to JMCY in accordance with terms.

Platform Operations & Commission Policy

Revenue Split Framework

By registering on the JMCY Freelance Network, independent contractors agree to our unified operational transaction framework:

  • Direct Client Remittance: All customer payments are sent directly to the assigned therapist via GCash, Maya, or standard physical Cash at the point of service delivery.
  • Commission Parameters: Independent therapists agree to send a fixed 20% platform commission of each successful booking to JMCY in accordance with our system terms and conditions.
  • System Maintenances: This 20% network share powers ongoing digital infrastructure maintenance, marketplace server upkeep, administrative operations, and continuous brand lead-generation campaigns.
  • Transparency Pledge: The complete monetary split breakdown showing your exact 20% commission remittance requirement is displayed openly on your portal dashboard before you confirm and accept any incoming service request item.
